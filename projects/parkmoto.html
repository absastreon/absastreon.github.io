ParkMoto

A free motorcycle parking map for London — no accounts, no ads.

The work

London has thousands of motorcycle parking bays — but the data is fragmented across council websites, out of date in TfL tooling, or buried in borough-specific PDFs. As a rider, you end up circling blocks looking for the faded "M/C only" paint, hoping it still applies.

ParkMoto puts every bay on one map, with borough-aware fee rules built in, one-tap navigation out to Google Maps or Waze, and a lightweight report flow for missing, removed, or incorrectly-tagged bays. Corrections feed back into OpenStreetMap so the whole ecosystem benefits — not just ParkMoto users.

There's no registration, no tracking, and no ads. Location is used once to tag a report, never stored. It's just a map, it's free, and it works.

What it does

F.01

Every London bay, one map

Sourced from OpenStreetMap and enriched with borough-specific fee rules. Filter by free / paid / covered, or surface favourites.

F.02

Report in seconds

Missing bay? Removed? Incorrect fee? Three taps and the correction is queued — and optionally opens straight in the OpenStreetMap editor for riders with an OSM account.

F.03

One-tap navigation

Launch Google Maps or Waze directly to any bay. Designed for use with gloves on, at a red light.

F.04

Installable PWA

Add to home screen, works offline once cached, loads fast on cold-start — behaves like an app without the App Store friction.

F.05

Privacy by default

No accounts. No analytics funnels. Location permission used once per report and never persisted.
